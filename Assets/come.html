Getting there & Getting around

120 kilometers from Tbilisi, there is an amazing mountain and ski resort - Gudauri. You may not believe it, but in such a short distance from sunny Tbilisi, you can find yourself directly in the alpine zone. The snow here lasts for 5 months and is an ideal place for skiers, lovers of extremes and active recreation.

From Tbilisi To Gudauri

You can get to Gudauri from Tbilisi in just 2 hours. A large section of the road from Tbilisi to Gudauri is of international importance and is used by approximately 10 countries for permanent land transit. Therefore, even in the worst weather conditions, the road to Gudauri is open and is rarely closed.

If you drive north from Tbilisi, the Zhinvali Reservoir will impress you - blue, cloudy water in a big bowl of mountains. Its area is 11.5 sq. km, and the maximum depth is 75 m; In summer, this place attracts many vacationers.

Then you will pass the small town of Fasanauri. Here you can take a break, go to some tavern and taste delicious khinkali.

The road continues till you reach the Serpentine - this is where the last section of the ascent road to the alpine zone begins. On the left, you can see a huge, invisible ridge, which gets smaller after every turn of the tortuous road, and in a few minutes, you will be able to see this ridge from above.
