Geography

Gudauri resort is located in Kazbegi municipality, which is bordered by Dusheti municipality to the southeast, Akhalgori municipality to the south, Java municipality to the west, and the Russian Federation to the north.

The territory of the municipality is entirely mountainous. Gudauri is located at an altitude of 2196 m above sea level. Winters are cold and dry, and summers are cool. The average air temperature drops to 3.5 Â°C, and precipitation reaches 1160 mm.

The municipality is rich in rivers, lakes, glaciers and mineral springs. In the vicinity of Gudauri, the Kelitsadi lake and the Ereto waterfall are worth mentioning. Ereti waterfall is 45 meters high. The rivers here are short, but they are characterized by fast currents and clear water. Some rivers have quite high and beautiful waterfalls.

The terrain of Kazbegi municipality is mountainous, mostly rocky and difficult to access. After Gudauri, on the military road of Georgia there is the most important pass - Jvari, at an altitude of 2379 meters above sea level.
